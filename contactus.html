Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common solar installation queries.

How long does a typical installation take?

A standard residential rooftop installation is normally completed within 1 to 3 days once the equipment is on-site. Larger agricultural or industrial farm systems can take between 5 to 10 working days.

Can I start with a small system and upgrade later?

Absolutely! Steven designs systems with scalability in mind. We can install a basic inverter and solar panel setup now, and expand your battery storage or add more panels as your energy needs or budget grow.

What maintenance does my solar system require?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Washing them every few months to remove dust or debris is usually enough to keep them operating at peak performance. Steven also provides routine check-ups and diagnostic support.
